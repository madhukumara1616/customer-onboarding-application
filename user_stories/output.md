EPIC: Customer Onboarding (Personal and Contract Details)

US-001 — Start Customer Onboarding and View Data Capture Form

As a prospective customer
I want to open the customer onboarding screen
So that I can provide my details to begin onboarding

Fields:
• Title
• Full Name
• Date of Birth
• Nationality
• Marital Status
• Phone No
• Email Id
• Residency Status
• UK Residence Status
• Visa Type & Expiry
• Years in UK
• Are you a servicemember or veteran? (Yes/No)
• Proof Document

Validations:
• None

================================================================================
US-002 — Enter Personal Details

As a prospective customer
I want to enter my personal details
So that my identity information is captured for onboarding

Fields:
• Title
• Full Name
• Date of Birth
• Nationality
• Marital Status

Validations:
• Date of Birth must be a valid date

================================================================================
US-003 — Enter Contract Contact Details

As a prospective customer
I want to enter my contact details
So that I can be contacted regarding my onboarding

Fields:
• Phone No
• Email Id

Validations:
• Email Id must be in a valid email format

================================================================================
US-004 — Provide Residency and Visa Details

As a prospective customer
I want to provide my residency and visa information
So that my eligibility and status can be assessed during onboarding

Fields:
• Residency Status
• UK Residence Status
• Visa Type & Expiry
• Years in UK

Validations:
• Visa Type & Expiry must be a valid date for expiry
• Years in UK must be a number

================================================================================
US-005 — Indicate Service Member or Veteran Status

As a prospective customer
I want to answer whether I am a servicemember or veteran
So that my service status is recorded for onboarding

Fields:
• Are you a servicemember or veteran? (Yes/No)

Validations:
• Value must be either Yes or No

As a prospective customer

So that I can provide supporting evidence for my onboarding details

Fields:
• Proof Document
